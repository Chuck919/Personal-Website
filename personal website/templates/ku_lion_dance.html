About Lion Dance

Lion Dance is a traditional Chinese cultural performance often performed during Chinese New Year celebrations, other cultural holidays, and important events. The lion dance symbolizes good luck, prosperity, and warding off evil spirits.

KU Lion Dance

As President of KU Lion Dance, our focus is to bring cultural awareness and appreciation to the University of Kansas and the Kansas community. We perform every year at the multiple different KU events as well as cultural events outside of KU. We have performed at weddings, Nelson Atkins Museum, local high schools, and many more.

About Us

We are a group of passionate and commited individuals trying to display our culture to the world. We hold practice twice a week, and focus our time on learning new tricks and creating new routines. As a growing club, we would love your support in helping us reach our goals! If you are interested in booking us for an event or have any questions, please feel free to contact us below!
